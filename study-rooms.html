Study Rooms

About Our Study Rooms

Our study rooms provide a quiet and comfortable environment for students to focus on their academic work. These rooms are equipped with the necessary amenities to enhance your study experience.

How to Reserve a Study Room

Reserving a study room is simple. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Visit the library's study room reservation page.
  • Select the desired date and time for your reservation.
  • Fill in your details and confirm the reservation.
