Vision

To become a center of excellence in engineering education and research and produce globally competent professional minds upholding human values and ethics to serve the society by taking part in industrial, scientific innovation, academic and entrepreneurship activities within the country and abroad.

Mission

The mission of the Faculty of Engineering is to: deliver quality education and achieve excellence in teaching-learning and research activities through the state-of- the art, high technology engineering educational programs. provide students the innovative and entrepreneurial skills to meet the need of the industry and society, and make them able to compete successfully in a global economy. build a culture of collaborative efforts and facilitate effective interactions of engineering students, faculty and management with the industry personnel, alumni, academicians of premier institutions and local, national and international community. develop partnership with the government institutions, private sector, and academic institutions in Nepal and abroad specifically to enhance teaching-learning activities, research, mutual exchange programs and creation of new engineering knowledge and technology transfer. achieve excellence in application-oriented research in the selected areas of technology to contribute to the development of the Far-western region and the nation.

Message from the Vice-Chancellor

On behalf of the Faculty of Engineering (FoE), I would like to welcome you at the Far-western University. The FoE has come a long way since the establishment of the University in 2010. Today, the Faculty is a vibrant entity where enrollment is growing, infrastructure (labs and equipment) is improving, and the programs are receiving national and international attention. This incredible growth is expected to continue in future too. It has been a leader in engineering education in the Far-western region of Nepal and aims to build educational programs that prepare its students to make difference in the world by contributing towards societal development. Now, it has been offering Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering and Computer Engineering in its School of Engineering at Mahendranagar. We also plan to start Bachelor of Architecture from the academic year 2024. With its aim to become a leading institution for higher education in engineering field, the FoE has also started master's degree in construction project management (MSc CPM) from 2023 and will start Structural Engineering from 2025 at its School of Engineering.Students from diverse geographical regions of the country are admitted pursuing engineering education at the FoE. Students get admission through competitive entrance examination conducted by the FoE. Despite the lowest fee structure among the Engineering institutions in Nepal, we provide quality education and the best academic environment to the students with the support of its young, dynamic and well-educated faculties. To promote sharing of knowledge, research and innovative ideas, and enrich students learning process, we invite scholars and professionals from around the globe and encourage our students to participate in the seminars and discussions. In addition, we have proposed to establish a Centre for Engineering Research and Innovation at our Central Campus to fulfill the need of carrying out research, development and innovation. The FoE has also been attempting to tie up with the international Universities to promote mutual collaboration on research and education. We encourage our faculty members and students to conduct research and innovative work in engineering field.Indeed, success in the engineering field requires more than a strong technical knowledge and the capacity to apply it to real-world problems. It also requires the ability to engage in close collaboration, as well as qualities like integrity, curiosity, creativity, open-mindedness and a willingness to engage in innovation. I encourage the students to enhance your academic experience by being fully engaged with your professors, your peers and the staff who are here to support you throughout your educational journey. I welcome you at the Faculty of Engineering to pursue your degrees and actively participate in research and innovation activities. Warmest Regards Assoc. Prof. Kishan Datta Bhatta, PhD. Dean Faculty of Engineering, Far-Western University Mahendranagar, Kanchanpur, Nepal We look forward to welcoming you!
