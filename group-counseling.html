Group Counseling

About Group Counseling

Our group counseling sessions provide a supportive environment where students can share experiences and learn from each other. These sessions are facilitated by professional counselors who ensure a safe and confidential space for all participants.

Benefits of Group Counseling

Engaging in group counseling offers various benefits, including:

  • Peer support and understanding
  • Learning new coping strategies
  • Improved communication skills
  • Emotional and mental well-being

How to Join

Joining our group counseling sessions is simple. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Complete the registration form.
  • Attend an introductory session.
  • Participate in regular group meetings.
