About Ask a Librarian

Our "Ask a Librarian" service provides students with direct access to professional librarians who can assist with research, finding resources, and answering any library-related questions.

How to Use the Service

Using our "Ask a Librarian" service is simple. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Submit your question through the online form.
  • Include any relevant details or context for your query.
  • Wait for a response from one of our librarians.
