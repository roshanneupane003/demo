Peer Support

About Peer Support

Our peer support program is designed to help students connect with fellow peers who can offer guidance, support, and a listening ear. Whether you're dealing with academic pressures or personal issues, our peer mentors are here to assist you.

Benefits of Peer Support

Engaging in peer support offers various benefits, including:

  • Emotional support
  • Academic advice
  • Social connections
  • Improved well-being

How to Join

Joining our peer support program is simple. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Complete the application form.
  • Attend an orientation session.
  • Get matched with a peer mentor.
