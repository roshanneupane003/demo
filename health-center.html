Health Center

Medical Services

Our Health Center provides comprehensive medical services, including general check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment of minor illnesses and injuries. Our team of healthcare professionals is dedicated to promoting the health and well-being of our students.

Mental Health Support

We offer mental health support services, including counseling and therapy, to help students manage stress, anxiety, and other mental health concerns. Our confidential services are designed to provide a safe and supportive environment.
