International Journal on Engineering Technology (InJET)

Kantipur Engineering College has been publishing The International Journal on Engineering Technology (InJET) since 2023. InJET is a multidisciplinary journal that publishes innovative research in engineering and technology. The journal is peer-reviewed, open-access, and indexed in Nepal Journals Online (NepJOL). Each research paper is assigned a unique Digital Object Identifier (DOI).

    focus Areas:

  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Architecture Engineering
